Technologies and Philosophy

My expertise and interest lies mostly in back-end technologies, using the python framework DJANGO. my front-end side revolve around redesigning templates on my taste by using HTML/5, CSS, JavaScript (as well as frameworks like jQuery, and little bit of react native). Right now I focus on getting my foot into open source contributing, as they open a world of possibilities and rewards for their hard-working developers. But most importantly continue learning and growing.

My philosophy on learning to use web technologies is always the same: If you do something without failing that's not probably the good one. Reading books will only get you so far -- you should spend time with your debugger, experimenting away with whichever technology you need to learn. Always keep an open mind, use tools you wouldn't usually use, and never give up.
